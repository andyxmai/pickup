About Us

Our Story

SafeDriven is revoutionizing how suburbanites can enjoy their evenings by bringing comfort and peace of mind. As an alternative transportation method, SafeDriven brings safety.

How it Works


Requesting a Ride

Click "Request a Ride" on the homepage and enter in your information. Our dispatcher pairs you with a responsbile and pre-screened driver. When you leave your evening festivities, our driver ensures you arrive home safely in your own car.

During the Ride

Let the driver know your address, and we'll take care of the rest! Sit back and enjoy a ride in your own car. Feel free to play any music, interact with your driver, or even lean the chair back and sleep. As long as your car is protected with insurance, you have nothing to worry about.

After the Ride

You and your car have arrived safely at your house. Our dispatcher sends our car to pickup the driver. The driver is then taken back downtown to help others get back home safely.
